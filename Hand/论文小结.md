
# PA-MPJPE

Procrustes Aligned Mean Per Joint Position Error, PA-MPJPE
Procrustes对齐的关节点平均误差(Procrustes Aligned Mean Per Joint Position Error, PA-MPJPE)：关节点经旋转、平移和缩放操作对齐后的平均误差

参考 [CVPR 2020 | VIBE: 基于视频的人体3D形状和姿态估计 - 知乎](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/133492544)
和  [CVPR 2023|ACR: Attention Collaboration-based Regressor for Arbitrary Two-Hand Reconstruction](https://github.com/ZhengdiYu/Arbitrary-Hands-3D-Reconstruction)
